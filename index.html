Geometric Intuition Reference Notes

I'm Vedanth Nayak, an incoming MMath & Phys student at the University of Manchester. I have a deep obsession for geometric intuition. I believe math shouldn't be a mechanical process of memorizing blind rules, but a visual language where you can literally see what is happening.

Before you read any further:

You do not need to buy these notes to learn this material. Every single concept in this shop is 100% inspired by and derived from Grant Sanderson’s incredible YouTube channel, 3Blue1Brown. Honestly, you can—and should—go watch his series. He is the gold standard.

I add very little of my own interpretation to these notes. Instead, I created them to serve as a fast, highly accessible, on-the-go reference tool. When you are deep in a problem set or coding a simulation and suddenly forget a core concept, you don't always have time to scrub through a video. These notes let you pull up that exact geometric clarity instantly.
